Jacob K. Amoah

Jacob K. Amoah is a dedicated attorney providing legal services to clients in Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, and Cape Coast. Mr. Amoah also assists individuals and businesses across the Greater Accra Region, Ashanti Region, and throughout Ghana. Before establishing his practice, Mr. Amoah was a partner at a prominent law firm where he focused on real estate, estate planning, probate, and business law. He has also served as legal counsel to local government bodies and held appointments with national legal authorities. Mr. Amoah is licensed to practice before all courts in Ghana and is committed to offering reliable and personalized legal representation.
